The Starbright Story

A little light in every cup

I'm the owner of Starbright Cafe, built with a passion for community, connection, and creating a space where everyone feels welcome. With a background in business leadership and customer service, I've always believed great service starts with genuine relationships and a strong work ethic. In addition to Starbright Cafe, I also own and operate a bail bonds company, which has further strengthened my commitment to helping and serving people in the community.

Starbright Cafe was created with the vision of bringing something special to Coffeeville, a cozy, modern space where people can start their day, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a great cup of coffee. Our mission is simple: "Shining a little light into every cup."

I believe in supporting small town communities, creating opportunities for others, and building a business that feels like home. From handcrafted coffee and specialty drinks to friendly faces behind the counter, every detail at Starbright Cafe is designed with heart.
